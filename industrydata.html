Ayesha UX/UI Designer Lead

Burning Glass data ranks UX designer 21st out of 25 on the list of top IT job titles in demand but does not display UI design. Highly ranked general skills for Ayesha’s ideal job include creativity (10th), research (11th) and team work/collaboration (5th). UX/UI was not displayed on Burning Glass data. However, UX design is an in-demand skill for the entire IT industry with a survey conducted by Adobe revealing that 87% of managers said this skill was a priority for consideration when hiring employees (Bradford 2020).
