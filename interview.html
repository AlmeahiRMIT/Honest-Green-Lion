Chitra Lele – Software Engineer, Founder and Head at Chitra Lele & Associates

1. What IT Work do you do?

Chitra Lele is a software entrepreneur, she uses her software engineering experience to create new business ideas which then becomes the base to the books that she writes. She is the founder of her own company, which is named after herself, it is called Chitra Lele & Associates.

As a business start-up owner and a software engineer her daily tasks consist of: At the beginning of her day, Chitra and her team have a short meeting session in which they discuss and assess their progress. This will help them to understand what has been achieved and what problems there may be, they will then put a plan into place. During the day she usually spends time brainstorming ideas, problem – solving, coding, testing along with some other tasks. Late in the afternoon, at least twice a week they have meetings with their clients. By the evening Chitra and her team try to have completed their daily goals by the end of the working day.

2. What Industry do you work in?

Chitra Lele is a Software Engineer. Software Engineers are people who build systems and software for businesses. These products range from business applications, to everyday apps, and to games. Here is some of the software engineer responsibilities:

  • Working with others to determine the Software’s requirements (What does a Software Engineer do,2021).
  • Designing and Developing a program or system to meet the user’s needs (What does a Software Engineer do,2021).
  • Create diagrams and models to help developers create right code for the program or system (What does a Software Engineer do,2021).
  • Document the system or program to help with future maintenance (What does a Software Engineer do,2021).
  • Recommending upgrades for programs and systems (What does a Software Engineer do,2021).
  • Working with other developers and engineers to create applications and systems (What does a Software Engineer do,2021).
  • Create advanced solutions to meet all technical needs (How to Become A Software Engineer – Salary, Qualifications, Skills & Reviews,2021).
  • Apply engineering principles to develop operating data and programs for computers (Software Engineers,2021).
  • Provide guidance and advice to client(s) (Software Engineer Careers,2021).

    • 3. What other kinds of work do you do?

    Chitra is a record – setting author, her software profession is used as the hub of her ideas for her books. She is also a peace ambassador associated with organization like World Peace Organization (USA) and Peace Writers Organization (USA).

    4. Who are all the different people you interact with in your work?

    She works with her team of software engineers; she also has weekly meetings with her clients.

    5. What are all your interactions with other IT professionals?

    Her interactions with other I.T professionals are usually limited to just the other members of her team.

    6. What are your interactions with clients or investors?

    Chitra Lele has interactions with her clients on a weekly basis, at least twice a week through meetings.

    7. What aspects of your work do you spend most time on?

    She spends most of her time working with her team, maintaining their focus on completing their daily set tasks by the end of the day.

    8. Which aspects of your work do you find most challenging?

    Software engineering can be stressful, to manage work related stress, Chitra’s company have a designated small area within their office where all employees can access for potential reflection during their break times. Given the unpredictable nature of her job, sometimes her work goes well beyond the normal working hours and she has spent times working throughout the night until the next day.

    9. What work do you do that best captures the essence of the IT industry?

    Software engineering is one of the most beneficial career paths for aspirants. No one is new or old within the field of a software engineer as this field is dynamic in native, it changes day by day.

    (All information about Chitra Lele – except from Question 2 – is sourced at: A Day in The Life of Software Engineer,2021).
