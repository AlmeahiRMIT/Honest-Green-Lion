He completed all his primary and secondary education in Mildura, attaining his VCE in 2001. He then moved to Melbourne, where he earned a Diploma in Telecommunications from RMIT. Matthew has completed multiple online IT training courses and has currently started a Degree in IT. Apart from his interest in IT, Matthew likes playing the drums. He has taken part in miscellaneous bands that have toured across Australia, releasing a number of albums onto the Interwebs and also thought the drums to high-school students.

AutomobileShare

AutomobileShare is car sharing platform that will allow you to lease out your vehicle when it is not in use. Think of Airbnb but for vehicles. You will be able to set dates for your vehicle’s availably, set prices, set age limits even approve or disapprove users from your vehicle. You will be 100% in control.
